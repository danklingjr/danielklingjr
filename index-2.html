    Personal Brand Expert

    Brand Consultant

    Stacy Phillips is a fashion stylist, image branding expert and brand consultant who has worked with some of the biggest fashion brands, celebrity clients, and television personalities developing the "look and feel" of their brands for national television audiences, as well as other multichannel platforms mediums. Stacy has extensive experience crafting on air talent and model images that audiences respond to and want to emulate. Among the numerous brands and designers Stacy has collaborated with include Isaac Mizrahi, Bob Mackie, Clinton Kelly, Lori Goldstein, Dennis Basso, Jennifer Hudson, Rachel Zoe, Heidi Klum, Nicole Richie and the Kardashians.
